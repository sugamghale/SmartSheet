How to Use SmartSheet

Step 1: Enter School Details

Begin by filling out your school's name, address, email, and academic year. These details will be used on the grade sheet header.

Step 2: Select Grade Category

After submitting school information, select the appropriate grade group: Grade 1–3, Grade 4–8, or Grade 9–10. You will be taken to a marks entry form specific to the selected category.

Step 3: Fill Out the Marks Entry Form

Each grade group has a unique form structure:

  • Grade 1–3: Enter the number of assessed Learning Achievements (LAs) and the total marks obtained per subject. Grades and GPA are automatically calculated.
  • Grade 4–8: Enter marks separately for Theoretical and Internal assessments for each subject. SmartSheet calculates grade points, WGP, and final GPA.
  • Grade 9–10: Enter marks separately for Theoretical and Internal assessments for each subject. SmartSheet calculates grade points, WGP, and final GPA.

Step 4: Generate Grade Sheet

Once marks are entered, click “Generate Grade Sheet” to instantly view a formatted, NEB-compliant result sheet. You can save, print, or share the sheet as needed.
